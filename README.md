## About

> ML engineer on DACON's DataScience team.
> Ranked 34 / 122,781 on DACON as a competitor — 1st in Genomic Breed Classification,
> 2nd in Court Judgment and HD Hyundai AI Challenge — now building the platform.

I got here the long way. Across '22–'23 I competed in eleven Korean ML competitions, team-leading six of them, and ended with an overall DACON rank of **34 out of 122,781 participants**. After graduating from Kookmin University's AI Big Data & Management program and a six-month stint at Suresofttech on AI dev & validation, I joined DACON itself in December '25 — the platform I'd been competing on — as a data scientist. What I care about is the full path: from the first EDA cell through a model that survives evaluation on unseen data, and now, through the infrastructure that hosts the competition that produced it.

<sub>DACON DS팀 소속 ML 엔지니어. 122,781명 중 34위 경쟁자 → 플랫폼 내부 합류. 리더보드에서 프로덕션까지, 그리고 그걸 만드는 쪽까지.</sub>
